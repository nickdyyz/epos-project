Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

EPOS Emergency Plan Operating System ("EPOS", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our emergency planning platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use EPOS:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and organization details
  • Organization Information: Organization name, type, location, contact information, and building details
  • Emergency Plan Data: Information you provide to create emergency plans, including hazards, procedures, and special considerations
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform

1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device information and operating system
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain the EPOS platform
  • Plan Generation: To create comprehensive emergency plans using AI technology
  • Communication: To send you plan updates, notifications, and support communications
  • Security: To protect your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • Improvement: To enhance our platform and develop new features
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Security

Your security is our top priority. We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your information
  • Password Protection: Emergency plans are protected with passwords that only you control
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use AWS cloud services with enterprise-grade security

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., AWS for hosting and email services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for processing based on consent

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Emergency Plans: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Retained as required by applicable laws

7. International Data Transfers

EPOS operates globally and may transfer your information to countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Other appropriate safeguards as required by law

8. Children's Privacy

EPOS is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform
